Guide to the Lady Margaret School Clubs Tool

üëã We're lucky to have so many free clubs available at LMS! It can be tricky to navigate them all, so the PTA and class reps have put together this quick guide to help you use the LMS Clubs tool. It's designed to make finding clubs and printing a timetable of your available and chosen ones super easy!


Step 1: Filter by Year Group

The first and most important step is to select your year group. You'll see a dropdown menu right at the top of the page. Once you choose your year, the page will automatically refresh to show only the clubs available. This is key, as it enables the rest of the site's features, including the ability to select and print clubs.



Step 2: Explore and Filter Clubs

Now that you've narrowed it down, you can start exploring!

  • Search Bar: Looking for a specific club? Just start typing the club name into the search bar.
  • Filter Panel: For more detailed searching, click the purple "Filters" button on the top right. This opens a panel with a few great options:
    • Day: Find clubs that run on a specific day of the week, like "Monday" or "Friday".
    • When: Filter by "Lunchtime," "After School," or "Before School" to fit your schedule.
    • Department: Want to see only sports clubs or music clubs? Use the "Department" dropdown to filter by categories like "Music" or "PE."
    • Club Name: You can also scroll through a list to see all the club names at a glance.


Step 3: Select & Print Your Timetable üñ®Ô∏è

Once you've filtered the list and found a few clubs you're interested in, you can highlight them for easy reference.

  • Make a Selection: See the small checkbox on the club cards? Check this box to add the club to your personal selection.
  • Print Timetable: After selecting a year group, a new button will appear: "Print My Timetable." Click this to generate a clean, easy-to-read PDF or send it to your printer. The timetable includes:
    • All the clubs available to your year group.
    • The clubs you selected will be clearly highlighted with extra details like the location and the staff member in charge.

A Few Extra Tips

  • Duplicate Clubs: The site will alert you if you select more than one club that takes place at the same time. This helps avoid scheduling conflicts, though it will still appear in your timetable if you keep it selected.
  • Deselect All: Want to start over? Use the "Deselect All Clubs" button.
  • No Results? If your filters are too specific and you don't see any clubs, click "Clear All Filters" at the bottom of the filter panel to reset and try again.

We hope this makes the club selection process even smoother!
