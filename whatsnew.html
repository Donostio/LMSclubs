Summary of Club Changes: January 2026

 

 Changes Affecting All Years

New:

  • Social Badminton (Monday before school, 08:00–08:30)
  • Dance Rehearsal Space (Tuesday & Thursday before school; Wednesday lunchtime)
  • Commercial Dance (Tuesday lunchtime)
  • Just Dance (Thursday lunchtime)
  • Basketball (Thursday after school, replaces rugby)
  • Dance rehearsal opportunities expanded

 

Removed:

  • Social Tennis (was offered Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday before school)
  • Bench ball (Tuesday lunchtime)
  • Dodgeball (Wednesday lunchtime)
  • Badminton (Wednesday lunchtime)
  • Tag Rugby (Thursday after school)

 

Continuing:

Band Club, Cricket, Football, Homework Club, Jazz Band, Lunch Club, Music Practice Club, Orchestra, Philosophy Club, String Ensemble, and Ukulele Club.

 

 

 Changes for Years 7–9

New:

  • Wood Workshop (Monday lunchtime)
  • Puzzle Club (Tuesday lunchtime)
  • D&T Club (Friday lunchtime)

 

Removed:

  • Explore Revise Discover (Monday lunchtime)
  • Junior Debate (Tuesday lunchtime)
  • Current Affairs Club (Thursday lunchtime)

 

Time changes:

  • Mathletes moved to Friday lunchtime (was Monday)

 

Continuing:

Art 4 Fun, Charity Club, Christian Union groups, Crafternoons, History Club, Jewellery Making, Junior Drama Club, Junior STEM, Scrapbooking, Sewing Club, and Wellness Room. Lower School Choir renamed to Junior Choir (still Thursday lunchtime).

 

 

 Changes for Years 8–13

New:

  • Contemporary Dance (Wednesday after school)
  • Psychology (Monday lunchtime, Year 10 only)
  • Politics Club (Wednesday lunchtime, Years 10–13)
  • Social Sciences (Thursday lunchtime, Year 12)

 

Removed:

  • Senior Debate (was Monday after school, Week 1 only, Years 10–13)
  • Literary Society (was Tuesday lunchtime, Year 12)

 

Changes:

  • Netball (Wednesday after school) is now squad (invite) only.
  • Netball (Monday after school) now has two groups: Year 7, and Years 8–13.
  • Extended Reading Group moved from Monday to Tuesday and Wednesday after school.
  • Street Dance extended to 17:30–19:00 (was 17:30–18:30).

 

Continuing:

Chamber Choir, Christian Union, Extended Reading Group, Football, Model United Nations, Netball, Orchestra, Philosophy Club, Street Dance, and Youth Alpha (Years 9–10).

 

 

Changes for Sixth Formers (Years 12–13)

New:

  • Pride Plus (Wednesday lunchtime, location B28)
  • English Scholars (Wednesday lunchtime, Year 11, invite only)

 

Removed:

  • Literary Society
  • Christian Group (Years 12–13) (separate from Christian Union)

 

Continuing:

Chamber Choir, Extended Reading Group, Model United Nations, and Social Sciences.
